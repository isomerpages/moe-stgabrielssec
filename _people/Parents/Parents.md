<h4><strong>Welcome to SGSS Parent Support Group.</strong></h4>
<p>Formed in January 2001, our PSG works in partnership with the school towards
a common goal of helping each child achieve their best. We meet regularly
at least once a month for discussions on program and events that promote
home-school-community activities. These include talks, outdoor events,
bazaars and lots more.</p>
<p>The PSG of St. Gabriel's Secondary School was setup with the long-term
on-going perspective of a tripartite relationship with the school, parents
and pupils.</p>

<h4><strong>Our Objectives</strong></h4>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Promote parental involvement with the school and its programmes by raising
awareness of school events and activities to parents.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Support the school and its programmes</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Build a better rapport with our children in their growing years by being
active participating parents</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Encourage parents to share their expertise and resources for the benefit
of the school community</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Foster a healthy home-school link</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Provide a feedback channel for the school</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h4><strong>Code of Ethics</strong></h4>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Respect and recognise that the Principal is ultimately responsible to
the school</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Exercise integrity at all times</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Set examples by being good role model for our children</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Put the interest of the group above self interest</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Encourage involvement of all PSG members</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Commit to the cause of the school</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Treat fellow parents, staff and students fairly and with empathy</p>
</li>
</ul>

network of links for the benefit of the school, families and students.</p>
