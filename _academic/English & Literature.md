<h5><u>Objective</u></h5>
<p>Every Gabrielite to be effective and confident in communicating in the
English Language.</p>

through the teaching and learning of English to enable Gabrielities to
be empathetic communicators, discerning readers and creative enquirers.

<br>

<h5><u>Programme</u></h5>
<p><strong>Lower Secondary</strong>
</p>
<p>Gabrielites are introduced to a range of literary texts in prose, poetry
and drama. They also begin to learn how to respond to and analyse such
literary writing. Those who are keen on Literature can choose to offer
Literature in English at GCE O Level at the end of Secondary Two.</p>
<p><strong>Upper Secondary</strong>
</p>
<p>Garbrielites will study the subject more deeply; delving into the literary
analysis of the texts offered. They will also be taught to hone and produce
sustained and critical responses to the texts they study.</p>
