<h2>NATIONAL CADET CORPS (AIR)</h2>
<h5>Beliefs &amp; Outcomes</h5>
<p>At St. Gabrielâ€™s Secondary, members of the National Cadet Corps [NCC]
(Air) Squadron go through a series of activities and courses planned for
them, for example, basic aero-modelling skills and knowledge, leadership
training camps, parades and many other meaningful activities. Through the
activities that they participate in, they learn values such as discipline,
care for fellow teammates and leadership. The Squadron also provides opportunities
for cadets to be immersed in other key activities organised by NCC (Land)
and (Sea) and selected cadets may even participate in overseas exchange
programmes to Hong Kong, India and possibly the United States of America.
The values, skills and knowledge acquired will serve them well even after
you graduate!</p>
