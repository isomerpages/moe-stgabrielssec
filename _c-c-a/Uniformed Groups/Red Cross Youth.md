<h2>Red Cross Youth</h2>
<h5>Beliefs &amp; Outcomes</h5>
<p>The Red Cross Youth Unit at St. Gabriel’s Secondary School was founded
on the 6<sup>th</sup> of August in 1966 by our late Principal, Reverend
Brother Noel. Our motto, ’Discipline de Dedication’, has been in use since
the unit was founded as the motto is our pride and with discipline, we
dedicate our commitment to the CCA. Over the years, the Red Cross Youth
Unit has been active in its participation in various competitions such
as the First Aid Championships and the Gold Modular Championship. It has
also groomed leaders to undergo courses and the Red Cross Youth Unit prides
itself in having achieved the Distinction Gold Award for the Best Cadet,
Director’s Award and the prestigious President’s Award which attests to
the high standards attained over the years.&nbsp;</p>
