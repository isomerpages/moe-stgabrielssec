<h2>Floorball</h2>
<h5>Beliefs &amp; Outcomes</h5>
<p>“ATTITUDE before APTITUDE”</p>
<p>Aptitude can be learnt, but attitude and beliefs are key factors in determining
one’s success or failure.</p>
<p>Therefore, at St. Gabriel’s Floorball, we firmly believe that the attitude
of every player is critical during their developmental years. Our floorball
programme focuses on the holistic outcome of every player, in and out of
the classroom. Through our close monitoring, rigorous training and playing
of floorball, we aim to inculcate values such as endurance, respect, teamwork,
self-discipline and leadership skills.</p>
