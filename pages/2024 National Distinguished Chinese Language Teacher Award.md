<p>The award, in its 27th edition, recognises teachers who have made an exemplary
effort to teach Chinese, as well as cultivate students' interest in the
language and culture through creative teaching methods and activities.</p>
<p>It is organised by Lianhe Zaobao and ZBSchools - the newspaper's website
for students - and co-organised with the Singapore Centre for Chinese Language,
the Society of Chinese Education Singapore, the Singapore Chinese Teachers'
Union and the Singapore Middle School Chinese Teachers' Association.</p>
