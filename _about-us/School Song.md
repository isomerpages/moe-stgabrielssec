<h2><strong>School Song</strong></h2>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 80%;  align = center" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/About%20Us/School%20Song/schoolsong.jpeg">
</div>
<p>
<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><em>In white and blue, boys full of mirth and life</em>
</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p><em>From early morn, we flood the green playground</em>
</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p><em>In every hall, soon starts the daily strife</em>
</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p><em>Of school routine, lessons and work all round</em>
</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p><em>With spirits bright, our will we'll gladly bracing</em>
</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p><em>From teachers kind we learn what's good and right</em>
</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p><em>We thus do hope to attain with God's blessing</em>
</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p><em>Virtue and Truth, Virtue and Truth</em>
</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p><em>Of life's true beacon light</em>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>
<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><em>Saint Gabriel's School Dear</em>
</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p><em>Here willing hearts behold</em>
</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p><em>When duty's call rings clear</em>
</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p><em>'Mid sorrows as through joys</em>
</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p><em>For Singapore and God</em>
</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p><em>For justice to uphold</em>
</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p><em>Let us live and thus prove</em>
</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p><em>Saint Gabriel's boys</em>
</p>
<p></p>
