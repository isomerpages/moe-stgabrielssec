<h2><strong>Education and Career Guidance</strong></h2>
<p>Education and Career Guidance (ECG) is about equipping students with the
necessary knowledge, skills and values to make informed decisions at each
key education stage for successful transition from school to further education
or work, and hence to manage their career pathways and lifelong learning
throughout their lives. Through ECG, social emotional competencies and
qualities of proactivity, adaptability and resilience are developed to
prepare students for the 21st Century.</p>
<p><strong>Our Belief</strong>
</p>
<p>ECG is the main motivating factor for students to find meaning in coming
to school.</p>
<p>In St. Gabriel’s, we aim to develop self-directed and resilient students
who seek continuous learning and growth.</p>
<p><strong>Student Outcomes:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Pro-active</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Adaptive</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Resilent</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Future Ready</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Our ECG Objectives:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To build confident students by deepening their self-awareness of their
interests, abilities and values</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To help students make informed choices of various education and career
pathways</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To equip students with future ready skills</p>
</li>
</ul>
